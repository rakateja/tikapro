Accounting Reports

Access real-time balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and trial balance reports. Make confident decisions backed by accurate, up-to-date financial data — without waiting for month-end.

  • Balance Sheet, P&L, and Trial Balance
  • Multi-period comparisons for trend analysis
  • Export to PDF, Excel, or sync to your ERP
  • Custom date ranges and department filtering

Bills & Expense Management

Centralize vendor bills and employee expenses in one place. From bill entry to receipt capture, approval workflows to reimbursement — track the entire lifecycle with full audit trails.

  • Centralized bill inbox with status tracking
  • Mobile receipt capture with OCR extraction
  • Multi-level approval workflows
  • Cash advance and reimbursement tracking
  • Vendor ledger and aging reports
  • Configurable spending policies per department

Daily Sales & Revenue Recognition

Record daily income across locations, categories, and payment methods. Recognize revenue accurately as it's earned — not just when cash is received — and reconcile effortlessly.

  • Quick-entry forms for daily sales and income
  • Breakdown by category, location, or payment type
  • Revenue recognition aligned with delivery or service completion
  • Automatic journal entries to your ledger
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly trend reports
